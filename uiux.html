UI/UX Design

About UI/UX Design

UI/UX design involves the process of enhancing user satisfaction by improving the usability, accessibility, and pleasure provided in the interaction between the user and the product. UI (User Interface) focuses on the look and layout, while UX (User Experience) is concerned with the overall feel and how users interact with the interface.

UI/UX Design Roadmap

  • 1. Learn the basics of design principles.
  • 2. Understand the importance of user research.
  • 3. Get familiar with design tools like Sketch, Figma, or Adobe XD.
  • 4. Practice wireframing and prototyping.
  • 5. Study human-computer interaction (HCI) fundamentals.
  • 6. Build a portfolio of design projects.
